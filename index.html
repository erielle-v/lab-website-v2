Shyam Prabhakar Lab

Laboratory of Systems Biology and Data Analytics

We Use Single-Cell, Spatial Omics + AI to Understand Human Diversity and Transform Disease Research

We believe that high-dimensional omics technologies have crossed a threshold. When applied to clinical samples at massive scale, and combined with careful data analytics and judicious use of AI, they can now reveal all of the mysteries of physiology and disease. We work closely with clinicians to ensure our work advances human health. We value and nurture every member of our diverse team.
