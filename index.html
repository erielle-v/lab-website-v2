We Leverage Single-Cell and Spatial Omics to Understand Human Disease

Our lab is an interdisciplinary team that combines single-cell and spatial omics technologies with computational and algorithmic approaches to study the mechanisms underlying human disease. We work closely with clinicians and domain experts to pursue ambitious, team-driven goals, while fostering a flexible and supportive environment for scientific and professional growth.
