S2GP Platform

The Spatial and Single Cell Genomics Platform (S2GP; previously the Single-Cell Omics Centre) provides the local research community with easy access to state-of-the-art single-cell capabilities, multi-omics expertise, and consultation. We offer multiple 10x and Fluidigm workflows (scRNA-seq, scATAC-seq, immune profiling [TCR, BCR], spatial transcriptome profiling). S2GP is a core facility that also provides training.

We're a 10x Genomics Certified Service Provider for Single Cell Gene Expression and Spatial Gene Expression Profiling solutions.
