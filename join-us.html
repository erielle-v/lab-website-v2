Join Us!

We are always looking for talented and motivated people to join our team. If you are excited about contributing to AI, machine learning, data analytics, drug discovery, cancer, or immune systems research, we'd love to hear from you.

Positions are available at all career stages (Research Officers, PhD students, and Postdoctoral Fellows) for both computational and experimental roles. We are open to candidates with a background in machine learning, statistics, computational biology, bioinformatics, or a related quantitative field, as well as wet lab applicants with an interest in single-cell or spatial omics technologies.

To apply, please email your CV with a brief cover letter describing your goals and background to prabhakars@a-star.edu.sg and shvetha_sankaran@a-star.edu.sg.

We're Hiring!

Research Officer (Wet Lab)

Intrigued by high-throughput data production and analysis? Here's a glimpse into what it would look like.

We're building a high-throughput platform for spatial profiling of clinical tissue, generating data that powers target discovery and disease biology research. We're looking for a Research Officer to help scale our wet-lab operations. You'll play a key role in transforming clinical tissue into high-quality spatial transcriptomics datasets, ensuring hundreds of samples move efficiently through the pipeline as we scale.

What You'll Be Doing

  • Run end-to-end spatial transcriptomics workflows on clinical tissue samples
  • Optimise workflows as sample volumes scale up
  • Maintain sample tracking and work closely with data generation and bioinformatics colleagues to ensure data targets are on track

What You'll Bring

  • A Bachelor's degree in Molecular and Cellular Biology, Biochemistry, Immunology, or allied fields
  • Experience handling clinical samples
  • Demonstrated precision in complex, multi-step wet-lab workflows
  • Adaptability in a fast-evolving environment as protocols and throughput continue to scale
  • Prior exposure to spatial transcriptomics workflows is a plus

Why Join Us

  • Work with cutting-edge spatial transcriptomics technologies
  • Work directly with clinical samples and see how the data gets used downstream

Interested? Write to prabhakars@a-star.edu.sg and shvetha_sankaran@a-star.edu.sg with your CV and brief cover letter.

We welcome questions, collaborations, and new ideas, so please get in touch with us!
